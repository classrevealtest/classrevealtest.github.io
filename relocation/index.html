FAQ
  • arrow_drop_downWhat's Class Reveal?

    Class Reveal is a webapp used to share your schedule with others in your school. When schedules come out on August 29 at 3PM, come back to this site to enter your schedule information and find out who's in your classes!

  • arrow_drop_downWhy a website this year?

    Mobile apps are not as accessible as an online, mobile-compatible website, not to mention the hefty developer license costs. This year, we are only using classreveal.com (the iOS and Android apps will not work anymore) in order to reach all students.

By using Class Reveal, you acknowledge that anyone with a wwprsd.org Google account can see the classes in which you are enrolled. We do not support non WW-P schools because of security issues. This year, we are required to verify that ClassReveal users are actual students, which is why you have to sign in using Google. We cannot verify students from other school districts, so we're limited to WW-P. Sorry for the inconvenience.
